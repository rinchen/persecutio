Historical Background

Catholicism arrived with French colonization, followed by Protestant and independent missions. Haitian Vodou blended African and Catholic elements, while 20th and 21st-century instability undermined church life and safety.

Sources: (2024) Open Doors World Watch List 2024

Modern-Day Situation

Gang violence and kidnapping target clergy, church workers, and congregations, while humanitarian collapse hampers worship, education, and relief. Priests and religious sisters have been abducted or killed.
