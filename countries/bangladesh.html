Historical Background

Catholic and Protestant missions operated largely among tribal and remote communities and later expanded urban ministries. Communal violence and new legal restrictions in the late 20th and early 21st centuries increasingly targeted Christian minorities.

Sources: (2024) Open Doors World Watch List 2024

Modern-Day Situation

Christians, especially converts and tribal communities, face violence, kidnapping, and pressure from Islamist actors. Anti-conversion narratives and blasphemy-related tensions put churches and leaders at risk.

Sources: (2023) USCIRF 2023 Annual Report - Bangladesh; (2024) Open Doors World Watch List 2024
