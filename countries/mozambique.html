Historical Background

Catholic and Protestant missions expanded during Portuguese colonial rule. Christianity grew significantly in the 20th century. The insurgency in the north has created a humanitarian crisis affecting Christian communities.

Sources: (2024) Open Doors World Watch List 2024

Modern-Day Situation

ISIS-affiliated insurgents in Cabo Delgado province attack Christian villages, churches, and humanitarian workers. Thousands have been killed or displaced since 2017. Church buildings are targeted and religious leaders face threats.

Sources: (2024) Open Doors World Watch List 2024; (2024) Freedom House Freedom in the World 2024; (2024) Our World in Data - Religious Composition (Pew Research)
